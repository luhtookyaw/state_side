### Hybrid Strengths

Hybrid shows its strongest gains with Easy and Normal clients. It has the highest Easy CTRS score (4.77), Easy WAI score (4.18), and Easy Alliance score (4.20), while also matching the top Easy Interpersonal score (6.00). At the client-level, Hybrid is especially strong on Easy Collaboration (+1.10 vs Baseline), Easy Strategy (+0.60), and Easy Understanding (+0.60).

For Normal clients, Hybrid performs best on relationship and understanding-oriented metrics. It has the highest Normal WAI score (3.93), Normal Alliance score (4.00), Normal Interpersonal score (6.00), Normal Understanding score (4.60), and Normal Collaboration score (4.80). Its Normal CTRS score (4.02) is also a clear improvement over Baseline and Adaptive, though Flash remains slightly higher.

For Hard clients, Hybrid is more mixed. It has the highest Hard CTRS score (3.49) and slightly improves Hard WAI over Baseline (+0.02), but it trails Flash on MITI and drops below Baseline on Alliance (-0.07), Focus (-0.20), and Collaboration (-0.30).
